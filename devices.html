Number of Devices

With internet connectivity becoming faster, cheaper, and more importantly, more versatile, the number of devices capable of being connected to the internet in one way or another is steadily increasing by the day. According to an IHS survey, as of 2017 there were over 20 billion devices connected to the internet all over the world (Srivastav, 2018). It is projected that this will rapidly grow as technology improves and becomes cheaper and more available. Along with a growing number of devices, there is a steadily increasing demand for IoT mobile apps that allow the aforementioned devices to connect to each other and interact with each other in new ways. This allows the consumer to customise and control their devices from anywhere at any time. This is the main reason that people are drawn to the IoT market, where customisation is the name of the game and the customer can configure their devices and setups to best suit their needs, the only limit is imagination and time.
